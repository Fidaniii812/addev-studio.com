Smart Home Air Tech 2025: Why Tion Devices Stand Out

Updated for 2025 • Estimated reading time: ~10 minutes

Air quality has become one of the biggest priorities for modern homes. In 2025, smart ventilation and filtration devices are no longer a luxury — they’re essential for a healthy home environment. Why Indoor Air Quality Is Critical in 2025

With rising pollution, allergens and sealed homes, clean indoor air is more important than ever.

  • People spend 90% of time indoors
  • Modern homes trap CO₂ and allergens
  • Mold levels rise without proper ventilation
  • Kids and elderly are the most sensitive

  • Very quiet — ideal for bedrooms
  • Energy-efficient for 24/7 use
  • App control and automation

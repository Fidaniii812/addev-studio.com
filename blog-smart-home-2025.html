Why Indoor Air Quality Matters More Than Ever

With increased pollution, allergens, viruses and energy-sealed homes, having clean indoor air is critical. Poor ventilation is linked to fatigue, headaches, sleep problems and respiratory issues.

  • 90% of our time is spent indoors
  • Modern homes trap dust, CO₂ and allergens
  • Mold levels can grow quickly without proper ventilation
  • Children and elderly people are most affected

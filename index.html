Workflow

How AdDev Studio fits into your affiliate setup

Think of this website as a central landing hub. You plug in offers, content and campaigns – and then send traffic from social media, blogs, or paid ads.

A simple 3-step flow

  • 1. Pick offers: Choose the brands and categories that match your audience.
  • 2. Link & tag: Use your tracking links from Admitad and other networks.
  • 3. Send traffic: Point bio links, blog CTAs and ads to relevant pages here.

Over time you can add more pages (niche blog posts, specific campaign hubs, seasonal promo sections) without breaking your structure or design.

Powered by Admitad Network.

Many of the offers you see here run through Admitad Partner Network, giving you unified reporting, payouts and tracking.

You stay in control: you decide which offers to highlight, how to structure content and where to send visitors first.
